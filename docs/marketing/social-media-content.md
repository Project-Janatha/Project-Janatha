## Platform-Specific Notes

**Instagram:**
- Use carousel format for Posts 1, 3, 4, 5
- Reels for Posts 6 and any behind-the-scenes content
- Stories: polls ("Have you moved cities for college?"), countdowns to launches, Q&As with the team
- Highlight categories: "About", "Features", "Team", "Community"

**Twitter/X:**
- Thread format for Post 1
- Single tweets with screenshots for feature spotlights
- Quote-tweet Chinmaya Mission content with Janata tie-ins
- Pin the launch announcement

**LinkedIn:**
- Longer-form version of Post 1 as an article
- Team spotlight with professional angles
- "Building Janata" behind-the-scenes engineering story

**WhatsApp/Community Groups:**
- Share direct app link with a short message:
  > "Hey! We built Janata — an app to help CHYKs find centers and events when they move cities. Check it out: [link]"
